Hi friends!

👋 I'm Ana, a US-based software engineer.

🧑‍💻 Since 2021 I work at Zendesk, where I build full stack features, API integrations and connectors, as well as write thorough docs and guides for what me and my team built.

🤝 Apart from the experience in technical roles, I have a background in teaching, music and humanities, as well as long history of people-to-people work. Communication and knowledge-sharing are my passions, which I apply to everything I do, including my engineering work: building software and docs with humans in mind.

Apart from my regular work, I'm a big fan of extracurricular activities, such as code club and book club at work, where I have fun connecting with people outside my team and trying out new challenges.

Before getting into tech, I used to be a singing teacher by day and a hotel worker by night. I also make music with an alternative fusion band Renelophus.

🫀 I am a big DEI advocate, passionate about bringing positive change to the future and increasing diversity and inclusivity in tech. To promote this and give back to my community, I volunteer as a site council representative on the ANZ site council at Zendesk. In this role I organize exciting events, drive initiatives that benefit employees from various underrepresented groups, assist my colleagues with getting educated on the variety of DEI issues and connect with the folks in a wider community. I really enjoy creating a nurturing and inclusive environments in every workplace I join.

🏝️ In my free time I like to cook, hang out with my cat Borsch, make music or paint boardgame miniatures. When the weather is nice, I like to get out of town for a hike.

If you would like to connect, work together or just say hi, feel free to get in touch with me on LinkedIn.
